NACOS CodeFest/Idea Pitching

Author: AbdulBasit Akolade (BASHMAN)
CODEFEST

NACOS FUO CHAPTER, did it’s first edition of coding competition and idea pitching program which was open to all levels and they all showed interest.

It was so interesting, where all students interested in the coding competition used a PYTHON environment for coding which the first, second and Third position was selected according to how best they did it.

Ariori Ibrahim came first and goes home with 50k worth of prizes

IDEA PITCHING

Another interesting eventis idea pitching where students brought up different ideas, illustrations and problem solving things to make life more easier and comfortable.

Students at various level showed interests and it came out entertaining, educating and interesting.

This is the first edition of the this great event. It was proudly sponsored by Meeqot, Frizan, 360Colonge, 1806luxe, Queen_Muthmahinah Accessories and Oyins Heaven.
